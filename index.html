Cooking Instructions

All recipes designed for after a long work day. Max 15–18 minutes each. Key tip for every dish included.

Scrambled Eggs

⏱ 8 min · Mon & Fri breakfast

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • ½ onion, diced
  • 1 tsp Rio Mare or olive oil
  • Salt + pepper
  • ½ lemon (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat pan on medium — add oil, let warm
  2. Add diced onion — stir 2 min until soft
  3. Add diced tomato — stir 1 min
  4. Crack 3 eggs directly in — stir gently pulling from edges to middle
  5. Take off heat when just set — still slightly wet
  6. Season salt + pepper · squeeze lemon if you like
Key tip: Medium heat only — high heat makes eggs rubbery. Take off heat while slightly wet — residual heat finishes them.

Tuna Rice Bowl

⏱ 15 min · Mon/Tue/Wed/Fri/Sun lunch

Ingredients

  • 2 tins Rio Mare (65g each)
  • 80g dry rice
  • 180g frozen veggies or spinach
  • 1–2 garlic cloves
  • ½ lemon
  • Salt + pepper

Steps

  1. Rice: 80g + 200ml water + pinch salt — boil then low heat, lid on, 12 min
  2. Open tuna tins — pour the oil into pan
  3. Heat tuna oil medium — add crushed garlic — 1 min
  4. Add frozen veggies straight from bag — stir 4–5 min
  5. Add tuna — break up with fork — warm 1–2 min
  6. Plate rice, veggies + tuna on top, squeeze lemon over everything
Key tip: Use the Rio Mare oil instead of adding extra — saves ~40 cal and tastes better. Cook double rice and refrigerate — next day's lunch ready in 5 min.

Chicken + Rice + Broccoli

⏱ 18 min · Mon/Thu/Fri dinner

Ingredients

  • 150g chicken breast
  • 80g dry rice
  • 180g frozen broccoli
  • 1–2 garlic cloves
  • ½ lemon
  • 1 tsp oil · salt + pepper

Steps

  1. Start rice: 80g + 200ml water — boil then low heat lid on 12 min
  2. Season chicken both sides with salt + pepper
  3. Heat pan medium-high + 1 tsp oil
  4. Add chicken — DO NOT move it for 3–4 min (forms golden crust)
  5. Flip — cook 3–4 min other side — rest on plate
  6. Same pan: garlic 30 sec — add frozen broccoli — stir 4–5 min
  7. Plate: rice + broccoli + sliced chicken on top + lemon squeeze
Key tip: Do NOT move chicken in first 3–4 min — this creates the golden crust. Moving it makes it stick and go grey. Rest 2 min before cutting.

Chicken Stir Fry

⏱ 12 min · Wednesday dinner

Ingredients

  • 150g chicken, sliced thin
  • 150g frozen broccoli
  • 150g frozen spinach
  • 1–2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp oil · ½ lemon
  • Salt + pepper

Steps

  1. Slice chicken into thin strips — thin cooks faster
  2. Heat pan or wok on HIGH heat + 1 tsp oil
  3. Add garlic — 30 sec stirring constantly
  4. Add chicken strips — stir fry moving constantly 3–4 min
  5. Add frozen broccoli — stir fry 3 min
  6. Add frozen spinach blocks — break up — 2–3 min
  7. Squeeze lemon over — season — serve immediately
Key tip: HIGH heat for stir fry — medium heat steams instead of fries. You want the sizzle. Keep everything moving constantly.

Egg + Tuna Omelette

⏱ 12 min · Tuesday dinner

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tin Rio Mare (65g)
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • ½ onion, diced
  • 100g zucchini, sliced thin
  • 1 tsp oil · salt + pepper

Steps

  1. Heat pan medium + tsp oil — add onion 2 min
  2. Add zucchini slices — 2–3 min until slightly golden
  3. Add diced tomato — 1 min — add tuna — break up — 1 min
  4. Beat 2 eggs with salt + pepper — pour over everything
  5. Let set on bottom 2 min without stirring
  6. Fold in half — 1 more min — slide onto plate
Key tip: If omelette breaks when folding — doesn't matter. Just mix it all together like a scramble. Tastes identical.

Saturday Meal Prep

⏱ 25 min · Do once a week

What you need

  • 400–500g chicken breast
  • 200g dry rice
  • Airtight containers x2
  • Check: eggs, tins, frozen veg

Steps

  1. Season all chicken with salt + pepper
  2. Cook all chicken — 4 min each side without moving
  3. Cool completely — slice — store in fridge up to 4 days
  4. Cook 200g rice + 500ml water — 12 min — cool completely
  5. Store rice in fridge in container — lasts 4 days
  6. Count tuna tins, check egg count, check freezer levels
  7. Write Wednesday shopping list for anything running low
Why this saves the week: Monday and Tuesday meals ready in 5 min. No cooking decisions when tired after work. Cool completely before refrigerating — warm food in fridge spoils everything faster.

The 7 Rules

Read these every Monday morning. Print them. Put them on the wall.
Rule 1
Eat
~1,800 cal per day. Protein at every meal. Plain yogurt daily. Rice at 80g dry per portion.
Rule 2
Drink
3+ litres water daily. Coffee before 1pm only. No sugary drinks. Sparkling water evenings.
Rule 3
Sleep
11pm target Mon–Thu. White noise app + earplugs. Phone on Do Not Disturb every night.
Rule 4
Walk
20 min after dinner at least 3× per week. Already doing 10 min to/from work — keep it.
Rule 5
Exercise
15 min home routine Mon/Wed/Fri — squats ×15, push ups ×10, plank 30s, walk on spot 1 min. 3 rounds.
Rule 6
Beer
Friday only. Max 2–3 × 650ml. Always eat a full dinner first. Alternate each beer with sparkling water.
Rule 7
Consistency
80% is enough. One bad day does not ruin the week. Do not quit. Keep going no matter what.
